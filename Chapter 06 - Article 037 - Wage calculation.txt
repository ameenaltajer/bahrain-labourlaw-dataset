Article 37

The wage of the worker shall be determined according to the individual or collective labor contract or the work regulations of the establishment. If the wage is not determined by any of these methods, he shall be entitled to the worker shall be paid the same wage, if any, otherwise the wage shall be estimated in accordance with the custom of the profession in the entity in which the work is performed. If there is no custom, the competent court shall estimate the wage due to the worker in accordance with the requirements of justice.
