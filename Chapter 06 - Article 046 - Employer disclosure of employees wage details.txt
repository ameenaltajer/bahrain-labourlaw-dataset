Article 46

A- The employer is not discharged from the wage unless he pays it to the worker in accordance with the mechanism specified by a decision of the Minister, provided that this mechanism includes procedures and controls for payment of workers' wages and the information that must be submitted to the concerned authorities to verify this.

B- Public and private entities are obligated to disclose to the concerned authorities information related to the payment of workersâ€™ wages to the extent necessary to verify their payment.

This is in accordance with the controls specified in the decision referred to in Paragraph (A) of this Article.
