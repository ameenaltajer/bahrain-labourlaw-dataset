Article 56

The provisions of Articles (51), (52), and (53) of this law do not apply to:

1) The authorized agents of the employer.
2) Workers engaged in preparatory and supplementary works that must be completed before or after the end of official working hours.
3) Guard and cleaners.
  
The Minister shall issue a decision specifying the maximum actual and overtime working hours and the overtime pay due to the two categories stipulated in the two clauses (2) and (3) of this Article, provided that this wage shall not be less than what is stipulated in Article (54) of this Law.
