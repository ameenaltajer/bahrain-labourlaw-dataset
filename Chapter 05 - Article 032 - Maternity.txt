Article 32

A female worker is entitled to a paid maternity leave such that:

a) The female worker shall obtain a paid maternity leave for a period of sixty days, including the period preceding and following the delivery, provided that she submits a medical certificate approved by a government health center or a clinic approved by the employer indicating the likely date the occurrence of the delivery.

A female worker may take fifteen days of unpaid leave on the occasion of childbirth in addition to the previous leave.

b) It is prohibited to employ a female worker during the forty days following childbirth, and her employment with another employer during maternity leave shall be subject to the provisions
stipulated in Article (62) of this law.
