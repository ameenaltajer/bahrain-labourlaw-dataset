Article 109

The employer is not allowed to terminate the employment contract due to the worker's lack or inadequacy of efficiency, except after notifying him of the reasons for the lack of efficiency and giving him an appropriate opportunity and period of not less than sixty days to reach the required level. If the worker fails, the employer may terminate the employment contract after sending a notice to the worker in accordance with the provisions of paragraph (a) of Article (99) of this law and in accordance with the regulations adopted.
