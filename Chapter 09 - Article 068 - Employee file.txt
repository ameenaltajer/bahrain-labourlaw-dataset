Article 68

The employer must create a separate file for each worker that includes all relevant information, including:

Name, age, national ID number, marital status, place of residence, and nationality.
Job title, qualifications, and experience.
Date of employment, salary, and any developments.
Leave obtained and penalties incurred.
Date and reasons for termination of employment.
The employer must keep in the worker's file minutes of investigations conducted with the worker and reports from his supervisors on his work performance according to the facility's work regulations, as well as any other documents related to the worker's service.

The employer must retain the worker's file for at least two years from the date of expiration of the employment contract.
