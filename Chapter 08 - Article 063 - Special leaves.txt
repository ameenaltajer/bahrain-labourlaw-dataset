Article 63

a) The worker has the right to a three-day paid leave in the following cases:

1) He married once.
2) The death of his wife or one of his relatives up to the fourth degree
3) The death of one of his wife's relatives up to the second degree.

b) The worker has the right to a one-day paid leave if a child is born to him.
c) The Muslim worker has the right to one month's paid leave if her husband dies. She also has the right to complete the death kit from her annual leave for a period of three months and ten days. If she does not have a balance from her annual leave, she has the right to unpaid leave.
d) The employer may assign the worker to provide proof of the availability of any of the cases stipulated in the preceding paragraphs.
