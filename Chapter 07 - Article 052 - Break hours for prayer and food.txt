Article 52

a) Subject to the provisions of Paragraph (b) of this Article, working hours must include one or more periods for prayer, eating and rest that are not less than their total is half an hour, taking into account that the worker does not work for more than six consecutive hours, and rest periods are not counted within the actual working hours.

b) The Minister may, by a decision from him, determine the jobs and cases in which - for technical reasons or operating conditions - work must continue without a rest period, and to specify difficult or exhausting jobs in which the worker is given rest periods that are counted within the actual working hours.
