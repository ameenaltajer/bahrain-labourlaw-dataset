Article 6

All labor lawsuits filed by workers or their beneficiaries shall be exempted from judicial fees.

The court shall rule to oblige the claimant to pay all or part of the expenses in case the claim is dismissed, without prejudice to the provisions of the Civil and Commercial Procedure Code.

The workers or their beneficiaries shall also be exempted from all fees for the certificates and copies they request, and for the complaints and requests they submit in accordance with the provisions of the law.
