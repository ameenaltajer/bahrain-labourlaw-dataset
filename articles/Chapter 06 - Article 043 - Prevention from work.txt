Article 43

If the worker comes to his place of work at the time specified to perform the work and is ready to carry out his work during this period and is prevented by reasons attributable to the employer, he shall be deemed to have actually performed his work and shall be entitled to his full wage.

However, if the worker appears and is prevented from performing his work by reasons of force majeure beyond the control of the employer, he shall be entitled to half of his wages.
