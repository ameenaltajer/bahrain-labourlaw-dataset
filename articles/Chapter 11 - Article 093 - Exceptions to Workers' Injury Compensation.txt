Article 93

Medical treatment by the employer will do not apply in any of the following cases:

1) If the worker intentionally injures himself.
2) If the injury occurs due to the worker's gross and intentional misconduct, including any act performed by the injured worker under the clear influence of intoxicants or drugs.
3) If the worker violates the employer's instructions regarding occupational health and safety, or negligently fails to implement these instructions. The burden of proving any of these cases is on the employer.
