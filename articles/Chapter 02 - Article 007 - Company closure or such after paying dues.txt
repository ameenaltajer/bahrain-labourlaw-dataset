Article 7

Dissolving, liquidation or closure of the establishment, in whole or in part, or downsizing of its activity or bankrupt it, merging the facility with another, or transferring it by inheritance, will, gift, sale - even if it was by public auction - or rent or other than that doesn't grant the termination of employment contracts in the facility. The successor shall be jointly responsible with the previous employers for the implementation of all arising obligations about these contracts.
