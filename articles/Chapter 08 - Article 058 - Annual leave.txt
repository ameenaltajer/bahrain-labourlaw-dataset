Article 58

Subject to the provision of Article (60) of this law, the worker who has spent at least one year in the service of the employer shall be entitled to an annual leave of not less than thirty days with pay, at the rate of two and a half days for each month.

If the worker's period of service is less than one year, he is entitled to leave in proportion to the period he spent at work.

The worker may not waive his right to the leave, and he may receive cash compensation for it in accordance with the provision of paragraph (b) of Article (59) of this law.
