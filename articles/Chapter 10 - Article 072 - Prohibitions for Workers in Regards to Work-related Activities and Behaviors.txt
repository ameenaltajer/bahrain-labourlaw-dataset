Article 72

The worker is prohibited, either by himself or through others, from engaging in any of the following actions and behaviors:

1) retaining any records, documents, or papers related to work.
2) working for others, whether paid or unpaid, without the employer's consent.
3) borrowing from clients of the employer or those who practice a similar activity to that of the employer, but this prohibition does not apply to borrowing from financial institutions.
4) accepting any commissions, gifts, bonuses, amounts, or other things in any capacity on the occasion of performing his duties without the consent of the employer.
5) collecting money or donations, distributing publications, collecting signatures, or holding meetings within the workplace without the employer's permission and in violation of the law.
