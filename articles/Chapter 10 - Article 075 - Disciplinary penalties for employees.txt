Article 75

The disciplinary penalties that can be imposed on employees according to work regulations and penalties lists in the establishment are:

1) Verbal warning.
2) Written warning.
3) Delaying the annual bonus payment for a period not exceeding three months.
4) Suspension from work with deduction from the salary for a period not exceeding one month per year, and not exceeding five days at a time.
5) Delaying promotion for a period not exceeding one year.
6) Dismissal from service in accordance with the provisions of this law.
