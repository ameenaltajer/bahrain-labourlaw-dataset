Article 65

A worker who has spent three consecutive months in the service of the employer and whose illness has been established and who is entitled to sick leave according to a certificate from a governmental health center or a clinic approved by the employer, shall have the right to the following sick leaves during one year:

  1) Fifteen days paid.
  2) Twenty days with half pay.
  3) Twenty days without pay.

If a dispute arises regarding the determination of the duration of treatment, the medical committee stipulated in Article (89) of this law shall determine this period.

The balance of sick leaves due to a worker may be collected with pay or with half pay for a period not exceeding two hundred and forty days.
