Article 51

a) Subject to the provisions of Articles (53) and (54) of this Law, the worker may not be actually employed for more than forty-eight hours per week.

b) With the exception of the cases stipulated in this law, a Muslim worker may not be employed during the month of Ramadan for more than six hours a day, or thirty-six hours a week.

c) It is permissible, by a decision of the Minister, to reduce the maximum working hours for some categories of workers or some industries or businesses whose circumstances or nature require that.
