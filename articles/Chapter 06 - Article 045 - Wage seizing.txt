Article 45

It is not permissible to seize the wage due to the worker, to waive any part of it, or to deduct from it in settlement of a debt, except within the limits of 25% of this wage. Raising this percentage to 50% in the case of alimony debt is permissible.

When debts are crowded, the alimony debt is given priority, then what is required because of the tools or tasks that the worker destroyed, or what was spent on him without permission.
A right or the financial penalties imposed on him.

For the waiver of any part of the wage to be valid, within the limits of the percentage stipulated in the first paragraph, it is required that it be with the written consent of the worker.
