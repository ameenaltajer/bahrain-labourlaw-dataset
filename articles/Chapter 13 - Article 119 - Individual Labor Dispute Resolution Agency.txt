Article 119

"Individual Labor Dispute Resolution Agency." The agency is responsible for resolving conflicts between workers and employers in a peaceful manner, and this can be done with the agreement of both parties before going to court. The Minister is responsible for issuing a decision that outlines the procedures, rules, and methods for settling disputes. If an agreement is reached, a document is created and signed by both parties as well as the agency representative, and this document has the authority to ensure that the agreement is followed.
