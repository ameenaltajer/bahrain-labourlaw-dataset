Article 20

The work contract must include the essential data of both parties to the contract, in particular the following data:

1) The employer's name, workplace address and registration number in the commercial register.
2) The worker's name, date of birth, qualification, job or occupation, place of residence, nationality, and what is necessary to prove his identity.
3) The nature and type of work and the term of the contract if it is for a fixed term.
4) The agreed-upon wage, the method and time of its payment, as well as all agreed-upon monetary and in-kind benefits.
5) Other data specified by a decision of the Minister
